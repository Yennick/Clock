SVG Clock with Raphael 2.1 (Part 2 - Drag-and-drop Time Picker Implementation)

This example uses default snapping, i.e. Dragging hours snaps to every hour, minutes snap to ever five minutes.

Drag the Hour/Minute hands, or change the values in the pulldown menu, to change the time.

Clock is in 24-hour format. Dragging the Hour hand in full circles to switch between AM/PM. Dragging the minute hand in full circles only sets the minutes within the current hour.
